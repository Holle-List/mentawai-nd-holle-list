The R code file for processing the digitised Mentawai wordlist (with
Indonesian and English glosses and part of the Holle List for the
Barrier Islands Languages of Sumatra, Indonesia) is available
[here](https://github.com/complexico/holle-list-barrier-islands/blob/main/processing-mentawai-nd-googlespreadsheet.R)
from [this
repository](https://github.com/complexico/holle-list-barrier-islands).
The digitised version of the Seumalur wordlist is part of the group
project in [this
repository](https://github.com/complexico/lexico-holle-list-barrier-islands).

## How to cite

If you use the data in this repository, please cite the original sources
([Stokhof, 1980](#ref-stokhofHolleListsVocabularies1980); [Stokhof &
Almanar, 1987](#ref-1885-144589))

> Stokhof, W. A. L., & Almanar, A. E. (1987). *Holle lists: Vocabularies
> in languages of Indonesia, Vol. 10/3: Islands off the west coast of
> Sumatra: Vol. Materials in Languages of Indonesia*. Dept. of
> Linguistics, Research School of Pacific Studies, The Australian
> National University. <http://hdl.handle.net/1885/144589>

> Stokhof, W. A. L. (Ed.) (with Saleh-Bronkhorst, L.). (1980). *Holle
> lists, vocabularies in languages of Indonesia, Vol. 1: Introductory
> Volume: Vol. Materials in Languages of Indonesia*. Dept. of
> Linguistics, Research School of Pacific Studies, The Australian
> National University. <https://doi.org/10.15144/PL-D17>

and the particular [released
version](https://github.com/Holle-List/mentawai-nd-holle-list/releases)
of this dataset ([Rajeg et al.,
2025](#ref-rajegDigitisedWordlistMentawai2025)), including the
interactive digitised master Holle List ([Rajeg,
2023](#ref-rajegDigitisedSearchableHolle2023b)), from which the
translations of the wordlist are computationally joined:

> Rajeg, G. P. W. (2023). *Digitised, searchable Holle List in Stokhof
> (1980)* (University of Oxford; Version 1.3.1) \[R\]. University of
> Oxford’s Sustainable Digital Scholarship.
> <https://doi.org/10.25446/oxford.23205173>

> Rajeg, G. P. W., Lamapaha, M. Y. I., Nazira, S., Putri, E. B. P., &
> Hendrayani, D. A. F. (2025). *Digitised wordlist for Mentawai (Pagai &
> Sipora) derived from Stokhof and Almanar’s ‘Holle lists: Vocabularies
> in languages of Indonesia, Vol. 10/3: Islands off the west coast of
> Sumatra’ from 1987* (Version 1.0.0) \[Dataset\].
> <https://github.com/Holle-List/mentawai-nd-holle-list>
